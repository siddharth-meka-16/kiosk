The Problem

Kiosks Were Not Built
for Everyone

Self-service kiosks power airports, restaurants, and retail — but they quietly exclude millions of users.

Visually Complex

Dense menus with tiny text overwhelm users with visual impairments or low digital literacy.

Zero Guidance

No step-by-step help. Users are left alone to navigate confusing interfaces under pressure.

Not Inclusive

Hearing impaired, elderly, and first-time users face silent barriers with no fallback support.

Payment Anxiety

Confusing payment flows cause abandoned transactions — especially for unfamiliar users.

The Solution

Adaptive AI That Guides
Every User, Every Step

01

Select Your Mode

Users choose their accessibility profile in one tap — Visually Impaired, Hearing Impaired, or Beginner.

02

AI Understands Context

The AI assistant tracks the current screen state and delivers precise, short instructions — never overwhelming.

03

Guided to Completion

From browsing the menu to confirming payment, the AI walks the user through every action confidently.
